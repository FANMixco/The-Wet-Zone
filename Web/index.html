About The Project

What is it?

How many of you have lived that any of our closest friends, relatives or acquaintances took the risk to go to the USA? I think most of us.

The Wet Zone is an App with a main purpose: "To Save Lives". This project was born in El Salvador, a country with high rates of migration due to different problems like economy, politics, among others. However many of the migrants whom take the risk to achieve the “American Dream” in order to improve their lives or help their relatives, suffer a lot of rights violations for example kidnapping, raping, stealing, and in the worst of the cases the death.

For all those reasons, we started to create this powerful App that works 100% Offline, to provide a simple tool to help the migrant (for those who travel by land). This App is not looking to encourage the illegal migration but it’s trying to keep the migrant alive. How? The Team has developed a Mobile App, originally for Windows Phone 8 Devices that supply some basic but useful options. For example:

1) I'm here!, This option provides your specific location and what is near to you for example hostels or danger zones, if the migrant have an internet connection, he or she is going to be able to share the location via e-mail with their contacts.

2) Map, is one of the most important options because provides all the information about danger points, embassies, hostels, water sources, among others, all the information came from different sources such as censuses of the United Nations, universities, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of El Salvador, UNDP and others (all the maps needed for example Guatemala, Mexico, USA must be downloaded before start the travel).

3) Profile, with this option the migrant is going to be able to create basic profile with their information, for example name, document, nationality, contacts, allergies, among others. The main purpose of these data is help the migration team if the migrant asked for help or is founded in a complex situation.

4) SOS button or panic button, it’s a very innovative option, that provides to the migrant an alternative to send a SMS to the migration office, police or rescue teams in order to ask them for help if they are suffering any kind of trouble during their travel. The SMS will provide a Map with their approximate location, in the future we want to expand the option to share the message to social networks like Facebook or Twitter.

5) Compass, if the migrant didn’t download the maps or the migrant is planning to use a paper map, this option it could save his or her life.

6) Survival Tips, these are some basic ideas of what the migrant can do during their travel, for example find water inside plants, check the Mexican’s Train Route, how to use the compass, among others.

How does it work?

The application works in OFFLINE MODE! Which means that the migrant just need to download the App and the Maps and it's ready to use it. As detailed above, we provide different modules.
