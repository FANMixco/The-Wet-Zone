About The Project

What is it?

The Wet Zone, is a project born in a country where there is a high rate of immigration, most die in the attempt or kidnapped and a myriad of other things that they have to suffer to achieve the American dream as it is known.

Knowing this, the project focuses on that and you can not avoid immigration help the migrant , in this case , illegal migrant , that alone does not facilitate illegal migration but keep alive the migrant , how? Simple, the project has developed an application that helps the migrant to stay alive in various ways, the first is a map that works in offline mode , preloaded with the points of danger in the way, collected from different sources as consensuses United Nations , Universities , the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of El Salvador , UNDP and others. The second is a compass that helps migrants to settle in order to know which way to go based on the preloaded map also has a system profile, where the migrant appends all personal data that will help rescue workers and security to identify the migrant to be the case of an accident or death on the way , a system of basic survival tips and SOS system will come to pass if you need something and give an alert for your help.

About The App

How does it work?

The app works in offline mode , which means that the migrant does not need a navigation package to make use of it. As mentioned above , has several modules.

The first module is a preloaded map with the location of various food stores , support centers , shelters, hazard zones and safe areas can be transported to a better and safer way.

The second module is the profile , where the migrant appends its basic data and medical contact if alg unexpected happens paramedics and other rescue workers can give better care and immigration systems be identified.

The third module is a SOS button in which case the migrant sends a warning signal to X target and thus it is easier for rescue teams to locate and safety.

The fourth module is a compass as any compass, orientation allows the migrant to know at what point you are and to make better use of the map.

The fifth module , are basic survival tips , which are prepared for the migrant can use pract.

The sixth and final module consists of a mobile support system in lies the meaning of each icon in the map by if the migrant feels any questions and brief instructions on the use of the App.
